Ancient Pokémon

Ancient Pokémon (Overview)

  • Ancient Pokémon include Fossil Pokémon and Ancient Paradox Pokémon.
  • Fossils are prehistoric Pokémon revived from fossils.
  • Paradox Pokémon are ancient-looking forms with special abilities.

Fossil Pokémon (Prehistoric)

  • Usually Rock-type or partly Rock-type.
  • Examples:
    • Aerodactyl : Rock/Flying
    • Omastar : Rock/Water
    • Rampardos : Rock
    • Tyrantrum : Rock/Dragon
  • Some fossils are different, like Dracovish (not Rock-type).
  • Archen and Archeops have no Hidden Ability.

Paradox Pokémon (Ancient Forms)

  • Come from the distant past.
  • Examples:
    • Great Tusk :Ground/Fighting
    • Scream Tail : Fairy/Psychic
    • Flutter Mane : Ghost/Fairy
    • Roaring Moon : Dragon/Dark
    • Walking Wake : Water/Dragon
    • Raging Bolt : Electric/Dragon
    • Gouging Fire : Fire/Dragon

Abilities

  • Protosynthesis boosts the highest stat in sunlight.
  • Paradox Pokémon have strong and unique stats.

Other Ancient Concepts

  • Super-Ancient Legendaries: Groudon, Kyogre, Rayquaza.
  • Shadow Pokémon are corrupted Pokémon from Colosseum/XD (not ancient).
